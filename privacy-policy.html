Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Entertainment Management App Documentation Website ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our documentation website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

The Documentation Website does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information. We do not collect any personal information that you directly provide to us through this website.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and your interaction with our website:

  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit, the time and duration of your visit, and the referring website.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you are using, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device settings.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies (small text files stored on your device) and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our documentation website
  • Understand how users interact with our website
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize site performance and user experience
  • Detect and prevent security incidents

Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to help us understand how users interact with our website. These services collect information sent by your browser or device, including the pages you visit and other information that assists us in improving the website. For more information about how Google Analytics collects and processes data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for their commercial purposes. We may share the information we collect in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting providers and analytics services.
  • Compliance with Laws: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with applicable laws, in response to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena or warrant, or to otherwise cooperate with law enforcement or other governmental agencies.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information to protect and defend our rights and property, to protect the safety of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: We may share or transfer information in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the information we collect. However, no security measures are 100% impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Documentation Website and Mobile Application are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Mobile Application Privacy

This section specifically addresses privacy practices for the Entertainment Management App mobile application:

Data Collection in the App

  • No Automatic Data Collection: Our mobile application does not automatically collect or transmit any personal information or usage data from your device.
  • User-Initiated Communications: The app will only send information when you explicitly choose to submit a bug report, support ticket, or feature request. In those cases, only the information you provide in your submission will be transmitted.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools in our mobile application.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your data is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Backup: The app uses Apple's iCloud to back up your data if you have iCloud enabled on your device. This backup process is controlled by you through your device settings and Apple's iCloud service. We do not have direct access to your iCloud data.
  • User Control: You maintain control over your data backup preferences through your device's iCloud settings.

Data Security in the App

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data within our application. However, the security of data stored on your device and in iCloud is also dependent on your device security settings and Apple's iCloud security.

Third-Party Services

The mobile application may contain integrations with third-party services (such as Apple's iCloud). Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

Links to Other Websites

Our Documentation Website may contain links to other websites not operated or controlled by us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice on our website or by other means as required by applicable law. Your continued use of the website after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

Your Rights and Choices
